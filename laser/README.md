## Lessons:

1. Keychain

- Make collage
- Sketch design
- Engrave design and cut outline

2. Geometric keychain

- Vector design
- Stroke, engrave, and cut design

3. Chess piece

- Combine raster and vector design
- Stroke, engrave, and cut design in two pieces
- Assemble pieces into base

4. SVG collage

- Design shapes with SVG
- Cut out of contruction paper
- Glue together into collage

5. Font design

- Design one more letters (vector or raster)
- Cut out and glue to block to make sorts

6. Tallest tower

- Make the tallest tower you can given limited amount of wood
- Must be free-standing
- No glue, adhesive, or extra material allowed

7. Light box
